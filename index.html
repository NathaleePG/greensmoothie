My Go-to Green Smoothie

My secret to making sure that I never forget my greens
(awesome for pre-workout)

Detoxifying, immune building, omega-3 fatty acids, protein building, and fiberlicious.

Adding superfoods such as chia seeds and flax seeds bring your green smoothies to a whole new level of health. Don’t skip this step if you want a breakfast richer in protective nutrients.

so health…

GO GREEN OR GO HOME

Recipe

1 ripe banana
1/3 cup blue berries
4 black berries (may substitute for strawberries)
5 cubes of frozen mangoes
1 tbsp chia seeds
1 tbsp flax seed meal
½ inch cube of fresh ginger
2-3 handfuls of baby spinach
1 & ½ cup unsweetened almond milk
1 scoop of vegan protein powder (optional)

How To:

The easiest way to make this smoothie is by simply using the best blender you have in the kitchen! Make sure to off start with the baby spinach at the bottom first, then just throw everything else in the blender. No need for ice causing any dilutions in this recipe since we already have the frozen mangos.
